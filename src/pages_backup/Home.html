Preserving Our Rich Bengali Heritage

For over 35 years, BANF has been the cornerstone of Bengali culture in Northeast Florida. We connect generations through our shared heritage, celebrating traditions while building a vibrant community for the future.

Pratibimbo - Our Annual Magazine

প্রতিবিম্ব - আমাদের বার্ষিক পত্রিকা

Pratibimbo, meaning "reflection" in Bengali, is our annual magazine that showcases the literary talents, creative writings, and artistic expressions of our community members.

  • Poetry and short stories by community members
  • Cultural essays and historical articles
  • Photography and artwork
  • Event highlights and community news
  • Youth contributions and children's corner
