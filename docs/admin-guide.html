All Portal Features

Dashboard

Real-time overview — total members, dues collected, events count, RSVP counts, and system health alerts.

Events (Evite)

Create events with name, date, venue, capacity, and ticket price. Send evite emails to all members automatically.

RSVPs & Attendance

View who has RSVP'd for each event. Mark attendance on the day. Export attendee lists.

Members

Browse, search and filter all BANF members. View membership type, status, dues history, and contact details.

Payments

Track dues payments, view payment history by year, identify overdue members, and export payment reports.

Automated workflows for dues reminders, welcome emails, and event announcements — runs on schedule without manual steps.

Tips for EC Members
A few things that will save you time.
Your session stays active

You won't need to log in every time you open this browser. Your session is saved locally. Click "Sign Out" in the top bar when done on a shared computer.

Bulk email uses templates

All automated emails (welcome, dues, events) pull from the Email Templates section. Customize them once and they apply to every future send.

Family records = CRM

Each member belongs to a Family Group in CRM / Families. When you update a family's address or phone, it applies to all members in that household.

Add new EC members via Roles

Go to Roles & Access to grant admin access to a new EC member. They'll automatically receive a setup email with a secure link.
